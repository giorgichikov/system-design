Design System

Buttons

Brand Button (Green)

  • Background: green-400
  • Hover/Active: green-500
  • Text: white
  • Disabled: 20% opacity
  • Border Radius: rounded-2 (~10-11px)

Danger Button (Red)

  • Background: red-200
  • Hover/Active: red-300
  • Text: white
  • Disabled: 20% opacity
  • Border Radius: rounded-2 (~10-11px)

Disabled Button (Any)

  • Background: gray-500
  • Text: gray-800
  • Border Radius: rounded-2 (~10-11px)

Inputs & Forms

Text Inputs

  • Background: gray-300
  • Background (Hover): gray-400
  • Text Color: gray-700
  • Placeholder: gray-700
  • Font Weight: medium (500)
  • Border: None
  • Border Radius: rounded-2 (~10-11px)
  • Padding: 27px left and right

Form Elements

  • Checkbox: gray-700
  • Eye Icon: gray-700 (password toggle)

Icons

  • Green Icons: green-400
  • Red Icons: red-200
  • Red Icons: blue-200
  • Gray Icons: gray-700

Badges

Green Badge

  • Background: green-100
  • Opacity: 20%
  • Text: green-200
  • Use: Positive status, success states

Red Badge

  • Background: red-100
  • Opacity: 20%
  • Text: red-200
  • Use: Errors, alerts, warnings

Typography

  • Headings: gray-900
  • Subheadings: gray-700
  • Price Text: green-300 (numbers only)
  • Standard Green Text: green-400
  • Register Text: gray-700

Colors & Backgrounds

  • Body Background: gray-500
  • Main Background: gray-100
  • Secondary Background: gray-200 (gray background on white background)

Links

  • Link Color: blue-200
  • Use: All href/anchor elements
